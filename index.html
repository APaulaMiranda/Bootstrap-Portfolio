About Me

I am very passionate about Finance, Technology, Strategy and how processes' optimization fits everywhere.
I have five years of experience in Business Research and Financial Services. I am currently working in Banking Operations at N26, the mobile bank the world loves.
I love to be exposed to different cultures and have always worked in a multinational environment and in different countries.
In my free time I love to run and to spend time in Nature (be it the countryside, the forest, the beach or the mountain) and to watch TED Talks and Documentaries. I am fascinated about whole plant based foods and how we can change the course of our lives through diet and lifestyle. I am very curious about the world in general. I love people - everyone has a story and everyone has lots of potential. I like team work and working for a common goal. I love how we can empower each other and learn from each other. It is amazing how we can transform a team to value more than the sum of its parts!
